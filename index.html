Vaulton Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 19, 2025

Vaulton ("we", "our", or "us") is a personal finance application designed to help users track income, expenses, budgets, investments, and financial insights.

1. Information We Collect

Account data: Email address and authentication credentials, used solely for account management.

Financial data: Income, expenses, budgets, and investment entries — all entered manually by you. Vaulton does not connect to bank accounts and never collects banking credentials.

AI queries: Text and voice input used to generate financial insights, processed by OpenAI, L.L.C. under their privacy policy.

Subscription data: Subscription status and plan type, managed via Apple App Store and RevenueCat, Inc.

Diagnostics: App feature usage, crash reports, and device/OS version for performance improvement only.

2. What We Do Not Collect

We do not collect location data, contacts, photos, messages, browsing history, or any data for advertising purposes. We do not sell your data.

3. How We Use Your Information

To provide app features, manage accounts and subscriptions, improve performance, and respond to support requests. Never for advertising.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your data. Data is shared only with:

  • Apple App Store — subscription processing
  • RevenueCat, Inc. — subscription management
  • OpenAI, L.L.C. — When you use the AI Financial Assistant feature, your query text and relevant financial transaction data are sent to OpenAI to generate personalized insights. Your explicit consent is requested before any data is shared. OpenAI processes this data under their own privacy policy at openai.com/privacy.
  • Railway — app hosting and infrastructure

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical measures to protect your data against unauthorized access or misuse.

6. Data Retention

Data is retained only as long as necessary to provide app functionality. You may request deletion at any time.

7. Your Rights

You may access, correct, or request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page.
