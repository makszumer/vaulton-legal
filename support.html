❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I cancel my subscription?
Open the Settings app on your iPhone → tap your name → Subscriptions → Vaulton → Cancel Subscription.

How do I delete my account?
Open Vaulton → go to Profile → Settings → Delete Account. This will permanently remove all your data.

My data disappeared after reinstalling. Why?
Vaulton stores your data on our secure servers linked to your account. Make sure you're signed in with the same email you used originally.

How does the free trial work?
Vaulton offers a 3-day free trial of Premium. You won't be charged until the trial ends. You can cancel anytime before the trial expires.

Is my financial data secure?
Yes. Vaulton does not connect to your bank. All financial data is entered manually by you and stored securely on our servers.

The app is not working correctly. What should I do?
Try closing and reopening the app. If the issue persists, delete and reinstall the app, then sign back in. If the problem continues, contact us at support@vaulton.pro.
